BODY

Know your parts!





Today, we're going on an exciting journey to explore the incredible world of "My Body."
Your body is like a super-duper machine, and we're going to learn all about its amazing parts and how they work together.

1. Meet Your Body:
Think of your body as your own personal superhero suit.

It has special parts that help you do all sorts of fantastic things, like running, jumping, and even giving big hugs. Let's meet these awesome parts!

2. The Headquarters - Your Head:
At the tippy-top is your head – it's like the command center of your body.

Inside your head is your brain, the boss that helps you think, learn, and dream big dreams!

3. The Mighty Muscles:
Have you ever flexed your muscles?

Those are the strong parts in your arms and legs that help you move and play. Flex them and feel the power!

4. The Pumping Heart:
Deep in your chest is your heart – the superhero who never takes a break!

It pumps blood all around your body, delivering energy to keep you going.

5. The Wonderful Lungs:
Take a deep breath – that's your lungs at work!

They help you breathe in fresh air, and you can feel your chest go up and down. How cool is that?

6. The Helpful Hands:
Wave hello to your hands!

They're like your personal helpers, letting you pick up toys, draw colorful pictures, and share high-fives with friends.

7. The Happy Feet:
Your feet are like magic shoes that help you dance, skip, and explore the world.

Let's give them a little wiggle and get ready for an adventure!
