Welcome to our super fun numbers lesson!
Today, we're going to explore the amazing world of numbers. Numbers are everywhere,
and we use them to count, measure, and have lots of fun!

What Are Numbers?
Numbers are like magic words that help us talk about how many things we have or see. We can use numbers to count toys, friends, or even our favorite snacks!

Meet the Numbers
Let's say hello to some special numbers:

One: The lonely number one is like a single star in the sky. Can you think of one special thing you love?

Two: Two is like having a pair of shoes or two yummy cookies. Double the fun!

Three: Three is like a tricycle with three wheels or three colorful balloons in the sky.
